CULTURE OF HOLISTIC WELL BEING

Well-being is the experience of health, happiness, and prosperity. It includes having good mental health, high life satisfaction, and a sense of meaning or purpose. The overall well-being has four main aspects: physical, emotional, intellectual and spiritual. Take care of all of them and your life will be glorious.

ABOUT US


Bhaktivedanta Academy for Culture and Education (BACE) is a non-profit initiative by alumni of prestigious colleges like IIT, NIT and working professionals to help India move a step towards its vision for becoming a developed country by empowering the young generation through comprehensive value education programs. The members of BACE team strive to carry the following core values in their effort to shape the better future of the society.
